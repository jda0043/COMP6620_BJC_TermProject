Beauty and Joy of Computing

Established by the researchers at UC-Berkeley, the Beauty and Joy of Computing (BJC) is a computer science program that’s specifically designed to encourage and develop the next generation of programmers and software engineers at the K–12 level. Utilizing an innovative curriculum with a trained staff and an established set of lectures, labs, discussions, etc., the BJC is slowly becoming a universal success as it’s rolled out across the country.
